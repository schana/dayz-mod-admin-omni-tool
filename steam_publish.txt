[h1]SchanaModAdminOmniTool[/h1]

An Open Source DayZ mod that provides a tool for admins to use for nearly anything. It never wears out and most actions have been sped up considerably. Simply spawn in "SchanaAdminOmniTool" for your admins and enjoy the results.

[b]Features[/b]

Use as a tool
  * Lock / Unlock doors
  * Unrestrain
  * Skinning

Use to build, dismantle, and destroy
  * Doesn't require materials

Please submit bugs on the GitHub [url=https://github.com/schana/dayz-mod-admin-omni-tool/issues]issue tracker[/url]. There you will also find known issues.
